# Glue-like slime
Glue is a sticky, gooey liquid that stretches well. Creating a glue-like slime is difficult, but it can be achieved by devising ways to weaken the crosslinking reaction of borax.

## Materials per bucket (About 7L)
- 5g of borax
- 20g of baking soda (sodium Bicarbonate)
- 18g of fructose
- 250g of maltodextrin
- 500g of pregelatinized potato starch
- 1.5L of 10% PVA (polyvinyl alcohol) solution

## How to make
1. Prepare a powder mixture of all materials except borax.
2. Pour 5L of water into the bucket.
3. Put 5g of borax into the bottle, add approximately 400mL of water, close the lid, and shake for about 30 seconds to dissolve.
4. Add the powder mixture to the bucket while stirring the water.
5. Add the PVA solution to the bucket and mix well.
6. Gradually add the borax solution to the bucket while mixing.

## Shelf life
Two weeks
